HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we get from talented professionals looking to make their next career move in India.

Is there any fee for candidates?

Absolutely not. Our services are completely free for all job seekers. Our fees are covered by the companies that hire our candidates. Your career growth is our priority.

What happens after I submit my CV?

Once you submit your CV, our expert consultants will review your profile. If your skills and experience match an existing opening with our clients in India, we will contact you to discuss the opportunity in detail.

How long does the hiring process take?

The duration of the hiring process varies depending on the company and the specific role. On average, it can take anywhere from two to six weeks. We are committed to keeping you informed at every stage of the process.

Will my application be kept confidential?

Yes, your privacy is our utmost priority. Your profile and personal information will be handled with complete confidentiality and will only be shared with potential employers after obtaining your explicit consent.

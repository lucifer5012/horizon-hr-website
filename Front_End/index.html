WHO WE ARE

Your Strategic Partner for Human Capital in India

Horizon HR Consultancy is a premier recruitment and HR advisory firm dedicated to helping Indian businesses thrive. We connect exceptional talent with great opportunities, driving growth and success for both companies and candidates.

Our mission is to be the most trusted HR partner for businesses across India by providing innovative, efficient, and customized recruitment solutions that deliver tangible results and foster long-term relationships.

Our vision is to redefine the standards of HR consultancy in India by leveraging technology and human expertise to create a seamless and empowering experience for employers and job seekers alike.

We operate with unwavering integrity, a commitment to excellence, and a collaborative spirit. Our core values guide every placement, ensuring we build teams that last and careers that flourish.

Experience Recruitment Service Like Never Before

At Horizon HR Consultancy, we are committed to providing unmatched service. Whether you are an existing client or a new partner, you are always valued. Here's what you can expect when you work with us.

Industry-Specific Expertise

We assign recruitment experts with deep knowledge of your specific industry to ensure you get the best-fit candidates for your team.

Extensive Talent Network

We have access to a vast and diverse network of professionals across India, utilizing job boards, social media, and databases to find hidden gems.

Rapid Turnaround Time

Our streamlined process ensures that we can fill vacancies quickly and efficiently, saving you valuable time and resources.

Focus on Retention

We don't just find employees; we find long-term partners. Our rigorous screening process leads to higher employee retention rates for our clients.

Guaranteed Satisfaction

We stand by the quality of our placements. If a candidate leaves within a specified period, we offer a guaranteed replacement or rebate.

Success-Based Model

Our "No Recruit, No Fee" policy means you only pay when you successfully hire a candidate. Your success is our primary goal.
